COMPOSITION / SAFE SPACE:

- Eye-level camera, 35mm lens look, 3/4 angle, medium shot (waist-up) unless otherwise specified.

text, letters, subtitles, logo, watermark, signature, speech bubble, UI, lowres, blurry, noise, jpeg artifacts, extra fingers, missing fingers, deformed hands, bad anatomy, warped face, creepy smile, childlike face, over-saturated, messy background, cluttered composition.
